# Recurring Character Voice Cards

Status: **provisional/source-only**. These cards summarize reviewed English-source characterization, but they are not binding Russian voice decisions until they contain a reviewed Russian sample, reviewer, and date. Record relationship-specific changes instead of declaring one universal form of address.

## Strahd von Zarovich

- Core effect: effortless authority, intelligence, possession, and controlled threat.
- Register: educated, courtly, formally courteous; never florid merely because he is ancient.
- Rhythm: complete, measured sentences; short verdicts gain force from restraint.
- Avoid: camp villain laughter, constant archaic diction, shouting, crude sadism unsupported by source.
- Address: establish `ты/вы` per interlocutor and scene during the first reviewed arc in which direct dialogue appears.
- Evidence: `Act III - The Broken Land/Arc O - Dinner with the Devil.md:40-46`.
- Approved Russian samples: none yet.

## Marked comic or dialect voices

Blinsky, Cyrus, and similar speakers require individual cards before their first translated dialogue. Recreate communicative effect, class, rhythm, and humor; do not phonetically imitate a real-world ethnicity or accent.
