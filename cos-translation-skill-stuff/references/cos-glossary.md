## Things NOT to do

- **Don't transliterate when a glossary entry exists.** «Айзек Стражни», not «Изек Стражни».
- **Don't decline proper nouns into invented forms.** «баровийцев» (correct genitive plural of «баровиец»), NOT «барованцев». When unsure of a declension, leave the noun in nominative and rephrase.
- **Don't invent Russian renderings of NPCs not in this glossary.** Bring it to Simon; if uncertain, leave the English in italics on first mention.
- **Don't capitalise common nouns mid-sentence English-style.** Russian capitalises proper nouns, factions, gods, named institutions — not common-noun descriptors.
